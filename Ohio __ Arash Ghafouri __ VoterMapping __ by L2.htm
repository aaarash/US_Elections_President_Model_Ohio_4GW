HaystaqDNA team members established the first full-fledged analytics department for a campaign for President of the United States in 2008. In a high-stakes presidential race that could have been determined by a handful of votes, HaystaqDNA's predictive models served as the backbone of the Obama campaign's winning voter contact strategy - from the come-from-behind win in the Iowa Caucuses to general election victories in every key battleground state.

For the first time HaystaqDNA is now offering its expertise to the political community at large through L2 VoterMapping. The issues and values branches seen here are modeled data available for nearly 100% of voters.
